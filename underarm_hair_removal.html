The Technology

IPL-based hair reduction

IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) delivers controlled light energy to target hair follicles — reducing growth over time while improving skin clarity and tone.

Non-invasive, well-established, and gentle enough for regular use on sensitive skin areas.
