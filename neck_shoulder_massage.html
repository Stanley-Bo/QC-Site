More than just a massage

The Techniques

Five methods, applied in sequence

Each technique targets a different aspect of tension — together they work more effectively than any single method alone.

1

Acupressure Back Opening

Firm, targeted pressure applied along key acupressure points on the back to open blocked energy pathways, improve circulation and prepare the muscles for deeper work.

2

Essential Oil Meridian Massage

Our in-house essential oil blend is worked into the skin using long, flowing strokes that follow the body's meridian lines — promoting circulation, warming the tissues and releasing surface tension throughout the shoulder and neck area.

3

Myofascial Release & Trigger Point Work

Precise, focused pressure on specific trigger points and tight fascial bands — the technique targets the knots and adhesions that conventional massage often misses, releasing deep-seated pain and stiffness at the source.

4

Meridian Brush Assist

A specialised meridian brush is used to stimulate the skin's surface nerve endings and support the flow of energy along the meridian pathways — complementing the manual techniques and extending the treatment's effect.

5

Shoulder Girdle Stretch & Soothing

The session closes with gentle guided stretching of the shoulder girdle to restore range of motion, followed by calming strokes that settle the nervous system and integrate all the work that has been done — leaving the body feeling open and at ease.

FAQ

Before you book

Please read — some of these are important for your safety and comfort.

We do not recommend this treatment during the first trimester of pregnancy (weeks 1–12). If you are in your second or third trimester, please inform us when booking so we can assess suitability and adjust techniques where needed. Your safety comes first — when in doubt, please consult your doctor before booking.

Yes — please inform us of any current or recent injuries, particularly to the neck, shoulder, upper back or spine. This includes slipped discs, frozen shoulder, recent surgery, fractures or any condition under active medical treatment. This allows our therapist to adapt the session safely for you.

Please let us know about any nut or botanical allergies before your session. Some carrier oils are nut-derived (such as sweet almond or macadamia), and we want to ensure the blend we use is safe for you. We will advise on suitability when you WhatsApp us to book.

Please inform us if you have any of the following: skin conditions or open wounds in the treatment area, blood clotting disorders or are on blood-thinning medication, high blood pressure or cardiovascular conditions, recent fever or acute illness, or any other condition you feel we should be aware of. We always appreciate honesty — it helps us take the best possible care of you.

Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the shoulder and neck area. Avoid eating a heavy meal immediately before your session. Arrive a few minutes early so you can settle in without rushing.

For chronic tension or ongoing discomfort, we recommend starting with weekly sessions until relief is established, then maintaining with a monthly visit. For general wellness, once or twice a month works well for most people. Our therapist will advise based on your condition during your first visit.
