Our Philosophy

Where skin care becomes skin training

Queen College was founded on a simple belief: the skin can be guided — not just treated. With the right techniques, the right touch, and a consistent programme, your skin learns to renew itself from within.

We focus on collagen stimulation, gentle anti-ageing methods, and long-term skin health. Every session is designed to build on the last — so your results deepen over time, not just in the days after a facial.

We work quietly and carefully. No rush, no pressure — only a calm space where your skin is truly cared for.

Signature Treatments

Crafted for your skin's long-term story

Each treatment is chosen or tailored to your skin's current needs — and where you want it to be.

01

Anti-Ageing Facial

A targeted facial that works at multiple layers of the skin to visibly firm, smooth, and lift. Designed for early or advanced signs of ageing — with results that are felt, not just seen.

02

Collagen Skin Training

Our signature programme. A structured series of sessions that teaches your skin to rebuild its own collagen — for deeper, lasting firmness that grows stronger with each visit.

03

Brightening & Skin Renewal

Gentle exfoliation, targeted brightening, and barrier repair — for skin that looks clearer, more even, and genuinely refreshed. Suitable for dull, uneven, or post-sun skin.

04

Maintenance & Calming Care

A steady-rhythm facial for clients who want to maintain their skin between deeper treatments. Soothing, restorative, and always adapted to what your skin needs that day.
