What to Expect

Your 9-step lash experience

A careful, precise process that puts your eye comfort and lash health first.

1

Sanitisation

All tools and hands are thoroughly sanitised before we begin. We want you to see, smell, and feel the hygiene — it sets the tone for the entire session.

2

Makeup Removal

Any residual eye makeup, mascara, or skincare product is gently removed from the lash line to ensure clean adhesion.

3

Mousse Cleanse

A lash-specific mousse cleanser is applied to remove root oils and any remaining residue — essential for the adhesive to bond properly and last longer.

4

Lower Lash Isolation

An eye patch is carefully placed to isolate the lower lashes, preventing them from sticking to the upper lashes during application.

5

Absorb Residual Moisture

A cotton swab gently absorbs any remaining cleanser or moisture at the lash line before the adhesive is applied.

6

Dry the Lashes

A small fan dryer is used to ensure the lashes are completely dry before application — any moisture can compromise adhesive performance.

7

Brush and Align

A small lash wand is used to brush the natural lashes into neat, orderly rows — making individual isolation easier and more precise during application.

8

Lift the Upper Lid

A small piece of tape is used to gently lift the upper eyelid, allowing better visibility of each natural lash root for precise, accurate placement.

9

Final Check & Aftercare

We check the entire lash line for gaps or uneven placement, correct as needed, and walk you through simple aftercare — including when to wash them, what to avoid, and how to maintain your results.
