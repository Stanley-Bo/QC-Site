What to Expect

Your 8-step skin reset

Each step is carefully sequenced so the effects compound — by the end, your skin has been worked on at every layer.

1

Makeup Removal & Cleanse

Makeup, sunscreen and daily buildup are gently removed, followed by a thorough deep cleanse to clear the pores. A clean foundation is non-negotiable — it determines how well every step that follows will work.

2

Steam · Soften the Stratum Corneum

Warm steam opens the pores and softens the outer layer of dead skin cells, improving circulation and preparing the skin to receive the deeper treatment steps that follow.

3

Oxygen Detox · Bohr Effect

The core of this treatment. Active nutrient gel and a specialised treatment head use the Bohr Effect to increase subcutaneous oxygen — shrinking enlarged pores, improving pigmentation, and flushing metabolic waste from within the skin.

4

Penetration Massage · Drive Nutrients Deeper

Targeted massage techniques drive active ingredients past the surface layer into the dermis, boosting cell vitality and circulation while the skin gradually plumps and brightens under your fingertips.

5

RF Firming · Stimulate Collagen

Radiofrequency energy gently heats the deeper skin layers, stimulating collagen contraction and regeneration. Fine lines soften, facial contours tighten — you can feel the difference within the session itself.

6

Second Cleanse · Clear the Residue

A thorough second cleanse removes all treatment residue and the waste drawn to the surface during the detox steps. This ensures the skin is completely clean before the mask is applied.

7

Soft Mask · Repair & Lock In

A soft mask suited to your skin condition that day seals in all the active ingredients from the previous steps, supports barrier repair, and leaves the skin with a final layer of deep, lasting hydration.

8

Head, Neck & Shoulder Relaxation · Finish

The session closes with a dedicated head, neck and shoulder massage — releasing the tension that screen time and late nights store in these areas. You leave with better skin and a body that finally feels unwound.

FAQ

Before you book

The most common questions, answered honestly.

The Bohr Effect is a physiological principle describing how oxygen is released from haemoglobin to surrounding tissue. Oxygen kinetic technology applies this principle to skincare — using a specialised treatment head and active gel to increase the availability of oxygen in the subcutaneous layer. This accelerates cell metabolism, improves pigmentation and pore congestion from within, rather than just addressing surface symptoms.

The overall experience is comfortable and relaxing. The RF step produces a gentle, warming sensation which most clients find pleasant. If you have sensitive skin, let us know before we begin and we will adjust the settings accordingly.

No downtime at all — you can head straight back to work or out for the evening. We recommend keeping makeup light on the day of treatment to allow the skin to fully absorb the benefits. Apply sunscreen and avoid prolonged sun exposure for the day or two following your session.

This treatment is particularly effective for those who spend long hours on screens, have disrupted sleep patterns, notice dullness or uneven skin tone, feel their skin looks tired or flat, or want to prepare for a special occasion. If you have active breakouts, skin inflammation or are currently undergoing medical skin treatment, please WhatsApp us before booking so we can advise you.

Every 3 to 4 weeks works well for most clients, aligning with the skin's natural renewal cycle. Results build cumulatively over sessions. After your first visit, our therapist will suggest a cadence based on what your skin actually needs — no pressure, just honest advice.

Arriving with a clean, makeup-free face saves time and lets us start directly — though we will cleanse thoroughly regardless. Avoid a heavy meal immediately beforehand. Do let us know of any skin allergies, active conditions or medications you are on so we can personalise the session safely for you.
